Strategic opportunity — Economic Impact Report (March 2026)
Our newly released report quantifies for the first time that myeloma costs Australia $7.16 billion annually — one of the costliest cancers per person. Most of this burden falls on patients and carers, not the health system. This gives the tax appeal a powerful, evidence-backed story: every dollar donated goes towards changing that reality.
